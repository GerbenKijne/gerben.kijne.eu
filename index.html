About

For more than ten years I've worked at the intersection of product design and emerging technology, with one consistent thread: building tools that make a measurable difference in people's lives.

Today I lead the blockchain work at Giga — a joint initiative of UNICEF and the ITU — where my team develops applications that accelerate the transition to a world in which every school is connected to the internet. I trained as a Strategic Product Designer at TU Delft, and I care about the full arc of a product: from the human problem to the system that solves it at scale.
